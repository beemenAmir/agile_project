About Us


Furry Friends is a shelter that connects animals in need with the right people.
We work between those looking to rehome an animal and those wishing to grow their family.
people who want to rehome their animals bring us the animal at our shelter,
and we post about it on the website, so the people who want to adopt or foster will contact us directly.
